Gemstone Printers created by KoZ
Steam: http://steamcommunity.com/profiles/76561197989811664
Github: https://github.com/dkoz/gsprinters/

How to install:

1) Place the contents of the gsprinters-master.zip into your addons folder.

2) Customize the printers by editing the gemstone_settings.lua located in lua/darkrp_config folder.

3) Customize prices and menu settings by editing the gemstone_entities.lua location in lua/darkrp_customthings folder.
